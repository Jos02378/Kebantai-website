WHO ARE WE?
The Kebantai team is passionately committed in building a community by which people, not just athletes can tie up their shoes and play. The Kebantai team consists of three underachievers with no background in sports, hence why we made this website in the first place! Ergo people like us can enjoy the game as well. We’ve told you our story, now it’s time to tell yours.

MEET THE FREAKS!

Joseph Haryanto

"Hard work beats talent when talent fails to work hard," Joseph affirms.

The 18-year-old has re-invented the definition of what it means to be a programmer. Once an underachieving student, with the combination of his work ethic and willingness to learn, Joseph has helped his peers co-found Kebantai. His resolve? To make athletics a place for all.

Nicholas Theo

"I've been winging it my whole life; I don't know what's happening anymore," Nicholas proclaims.

Even though Nicholas, the co-founder of Kebantai, has a carefree attitude, his vision for the future, paired with his versatility in adeptness, has helped make Kebantai where it is today.
