WELCOME ABOARD!

Kebantai is the first ever platform where people can find matches and hone their sports skills in real time, on the real world. We are a sports brand that strives to bring people (no matter their background in sport) together while making meaningful memories.

FIND EVENTS NEAR YOU IN NO TIME!

The kebantai platform provides you with a polished automatic search management system with top-of-the-line yet simple features. So whether you're into dribbling a basketball or hearing the sound of a shuttlecock bounce up against the floor, kebantai's got you covered. Find what best fits your schedule, at a moment's notice, all in the contentment of your fingertips.

HOST MANY MEMORABLE EVENTS!

The days where it's required for you to have connections with a particular sport to play finally comes to an end. With kebantai, you can create and host your own matches in real-time, in the real world, where your status doesn't matter. So whether you're an amateur trying to learn the principles and groundwork of the sport you love, or if you're the captain of your school's athletic team who's trying to find another school to spar against, we've got it covered. Kebantai is an all-inclusive website that was manufactured for everyone, and you're just a few clicks away from unlocking your true potential!

CONNECT WITH FELLOW PEERS!

With kebantai's own personal chatroom and application system, we want to ensure that our users communicate effectively and efficiently. As the host, you get to choose who gets to join in on the fun. And as a member, you can apply to these match rooms through our user-friendly application system effortlessly.
